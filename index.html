About

She knows how to make the world envious of her with her attractive aura, eye soothing beauty, street smart skills, charming social skills and genuine love for everyone.

Always curious, extremely calculative, fun loving, friend's favourite, family's favourite, multi talented, ever jumpy, she surely knows how to live a life

She bagged the best sportsperson award in her school, she is a scholarship holder and the college topper, an admired blogger, an ecstatic model, a nature loving NGO collaborator, a mesmerising speed painting artist, who also flaunts her talent of curly hair care counselling. She now proudly hosts a finance based company's youtube channel in the most graceful manner possible.
